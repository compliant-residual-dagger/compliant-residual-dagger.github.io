System Overview

CR-DAgger consists of (1) a Compliant Intervention Interface that leverages compliance control, allowing humans to provide gentle, accurate delta action corrections without interrupting the ongoing robot policy execution; and (2) a Compliant Residual Policy formulation that learns from human corrections while incorporating force feedback and force control.
